Recharge Tutorial
1
Log in to your CryptoPro account
Open the CryptoPro app or website and log in using your credentials.
2
Navigate to Recharge section
Click on the "Recharge" button in the main menu or dashboard.
3
Select your network
Choose between BEP20, TRC20 or ETH network based on your preference.
4
Copy the deposit address
Carefully copy the provided wallet address or scan the QR code.
5
Send your funds
From your external wallet, send the amount you wish to deposit to the copied address.
6
Wait for confirmation
Your funds will appear in your account after network confirmation (usually within 5-30 minutes).
