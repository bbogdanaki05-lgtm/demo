Withdraw Tutorial
1
Log in to your account
Open CryptoPro and enter your credentials to access your dashboard.
2
Go to Withdraw section
Tap on the "Withdraw" option in the main menu or home screen.
3
Select network
Choose between BEP20 or TRC20 networks for your withdrawal.
4
Enter withdrawal details
Input the amount and your external wallet address carefully.
5
Confirm transaction
Double-check all information and confirm the withdrawal.
6
Wait for processing
Withdrawals are typically completed within 24 hours.
